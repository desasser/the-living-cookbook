# Instructions
1. In a sauce pan bring sugar, cocoa, butter, milk to a rapid boil for 1 minute
2. Remove from heat, add oats, peanut butter, and vanilla. Mix well.
3. Quickly drop spoonfuls onto waxed paper
4. Let cool
